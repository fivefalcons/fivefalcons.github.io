About Five Falcons

Built for dependable over-the-road execution.

Five Falcons Express Inc is an Illinois-based motor carrier focused on power-only transportation. We support customers, brokers, and logistics partners with timely communication and reliable hook-and-drop execution.

Our operating model is straightforward: arrive prepared, connect safely, communicate clearly, and keep the freight moving toward its destination.
