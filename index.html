What power only means

The tractor and driver—without adding a trailer to the arrangement.

In a power-only move, the transportation partner supplies the tractor and qualified driver while the shipper, broker, carrier, or another logistics partner supplies the trailer. It is a focused service with a clear division of equipment responsibility.

Tractor and driver

Five Falcons supplies the power unit and driver for an approved trailer movement.

Trailer supplied by partner

The trailer, freight readiness, pickup details, and delivery requirements come from the customer or logistics partner.

Details before confirmation

We review the lane, dates, trailer type, appointments, documents, and operating requirements before confirming capacity.

Frequently asked questions

Useful answers before you contact us.

Have a different question? Call or send the details through the contact form.

What is power-only trucking?+

Power-only trucking means the carrier provides the tractor and driver while another party supplies the trailer. The trailer may be loaded or empty depending on the arranged movement.

Who provides the trailer?+

The shipper, broker, carrier, customer, or another logistics partner provides the trailer and the trailer-related operating details.

What information should I send?+

Include pickup and delivery locations, dates and appointment times, trailer type and condition, whether it is loaded or empty, required documents, and any special instructions.

Do you guarantee every lane or date?+

No. Capacity is confirmed only after we review the lane, timing, equipment, weather, operating conditions, and other requirements.

Can I receive SMS updates?+

Yes, when you voluntarily provide a phone number and select the optional SMS consent checkbox. Message frequency may vary, message and data rates may apply, and you may reply STOP to opt out or HELP for assistance.
